There are two ways of earning on Ureed.com
Get paid per deliverable

You can choose to set a fixed rate per project. To set a realistic amount, take look at the client's brief, other freelancers' pitches and assess the time and resources needed to complete the project.

Get paid for your time

You can choose to set an hourly billing per project. To set your hourly rate, always consider the years of experience you possess, how much similar providers are charging and the time and resources needed to complete the project.

Service fees

We charge a 20% service fee off each transaction conducted over the platform. This percentage helps us ensure that we can grow our marketplace for both employers and freelancers and drive more employers to publish their boldest initiatives on Ureed.com.

We will be revisiting our fee structure in the near future to allow sliding fees for experienced freelancers.

Open for business

Refer your clients to Ureed.com and invite them to hire you via the platform to receive $10 USD in your wallet. All transactions with your employers will be free of service charges.

Why open for business?
Save 30% of your time by focusing only on your talents not the logistics. Ensure rapid and secure payments. Gain a centralized overview of all your projects and invoices. Boost your freelancer portfolio and gain more visibility!
