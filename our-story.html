Get Started
A homegrown marketplace from the region for the region

Created in 2017, Ureed.com is a freelance services marketplace seeking to provide endless opportunities for talents in the MENAPT region and beyond. Seeing that freelancing is becoming the new normal in an ever-thriving gig economy,

Ureed.com's founders, all nationals of MENA countries, soon realized that now more than ever is the time for freelance work to become better, easier and more accessible in the region.

With diversity at its core

From the very beginning, Ureed.com has been committed to diversity and inclusion. Being firm believers that talent doesn't stop at any border, Ureed.com's founders wanted to build a team from all over the region to make sure that all minds are represented.

Today, the company boasts of a team of inhouse professionals from Jordan to Egypt all the way to India and Pakistan. Together, they join forces from all corners of the world to empower talents of all countries to have access to viable economic opportunities through the platform, wherever they are and no matter what they do.

Redefining cross-border collaboration

In a fast-paced and highly technological world, flexibility is needed and necessary. We realize that for savvy businesses to perform their everyday activities with relative ease, outsourcing and freelancing need to be part of the picture. And this is where Ureed.com comes in. Whether you're a savvy entrepreneur, a business hustler or someone who just happens to dream big, the platform offers you the flexibility needed to innovate and pursue bold ideas through cross-border collaboration without ever breaking the bank.
