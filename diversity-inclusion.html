Marketplaces are meant for everyone

Ureed.com is committed to promoting diversity, inclusion and equality of opportunity-starting from the workforce that helps us build the marketplace up until the freelancers offering their talent on the platform. Since 2017, we've doubled in size and we've grown beyond any borders. Today, we have more than 28 employees working remotely from across 6 countries.

As a startup operating at this scale, we bring an elevated level of awareness, sensibility and responsibility to everything we do, from how we talk up to how we represent ourselves and communicate with the world.What this also means, is that as an online marketplace, we are constantly doing our best efforts to make sure everyone gets a seat at the table. Today, our network of freelancers exceeds 100,000 professionals and spans nearly 207 countries. Our freelance workforce is 55% women, with the majority of talents never taking no for an answer below the age of 30.

When actions speak louder than words
A mission to increase women’s participation in the regional workforce

In collaboration with the International Finance Corporation (IFC), a member of the World Bank Group, we are launching the training program "Mastering the World of Online Freelancing" in October 2020 with a mission to increase the employability of women in Lebanon and Jordan. The program's aim is to secure women with linguistic abilities in the region -who otherwise can't leave their homes- a sustainable source of income through the model of freelancing.

Through multiple courses offered by professionals on Ureed.com, we are aiming to help over 1,000 female trainees by the end of 2021 sharpen their talents and put them to use on Ureed.com. Opportunities with renowned clients on Ureed.com are to be cross-matched by UExperts to each and every woman who has taken part in the program throughout a period of 18 months; that is to ensure that female talents in the region are finding the right avenues and opportunities to increase their independence and participation in the workforce.
